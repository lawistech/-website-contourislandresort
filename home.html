About Our Resort

Welcome to Contour Island Resort & Spa, nestled on the tranquil shores of Banasura Sagar Lake amidst the breathtaking landscapes of Wayanad. Surrounded by lush greenery and serene waters, our idyllic retreat offers a sanctuary from everyday life.

Immerse yourself in nature's beauty, from mist-covered hills to dense forests and cascading waterfalls. Whether seeking adventure or solace, find your perfect getaway here to unwind and reconnect.
