Welcome to

Sunview Beach Resort

Sunview Beach Resort & Spa, managed by Citrine Hotels & Resorts is one of the leading Resorts in Varkala, is truly an exquisite place for your holidays situated at the amazing coastline of Varkala Beach, Trivandrum, Kerala.

Sunview remerges as one of the most captivating waterfront destinations of its kind following a landmark transformation. This iconic resort, which welcomed its first guests in 2001, inspires a new community of travellers today with remarkable sightlines, intuitive amenities, and emotionally rewarding encounters.
