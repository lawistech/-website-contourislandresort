Welcome to Vanya Holiday Resort

The Vanya Holiday Resort is a vibrant haven located amidst the lush greenery of the Nelliampathi forests. In addition to the magnificent views and soothing atmosphere, the resort boasts a range of modern facilities to ensure a delightful stay.

Away from the din of the city, this retreat amidst the hills takes you on a refreshing journey across one of the most beautiful destinations in Kerala. The ideal location for wildlife lovers, this captivating place seems alive with the symphonies weaved by its numerous avian inhabitants.

Wake up to the melodies of the birds, breathe in the crispy fresh air of a Nelliampathi morning, and take a walk amidst the refreshing greenery to get a renewed lease on life. There's beauty to be found in the simple joys of life, and Vanya can take you on a road to rediscovering your zest for life. Surpassing all others, the Vanya won both the SATA Award and the Metro Expedition Award for Best Jungle Resort/Lodge in 2022 and continued the streak by winning the SATA Award again in 2023.

Traveler's Insights

Discover the stunning attractions around our resort

Nelliyampathy Hills

Experience the enchanting beauty of Nelliyampathy Hills, known as the 'Poor Man's Ooty'. Explore tea plantations, coffee estates, and orange farms surrounded by mist-covered peaks and lush valleys.

Surrounding the resort

Malampuzha Dam and Gardens

Visit Kerala's largest reservoir surrounded by picturesque gardens featuring a rock garden, Japanese garden, and snake park. Don't miss the giant Yakshi sculpture and the ropeway ride offering breathtaking views.

40 km from resort

Parambikulam Wildlife Sanctuary

Explore one of India's most protected ecological areas with rich biodiversity. Home to tigers, leopards, elephants, and over 250 bird species, the sanctuary offers safari experiences and trekking opportunities for wildlife enthusiasts.

60 km from resort

How to Reach

Find your way to our serene forest retreat with ease

By Road
By Rail
By Air

From Palakkad

Vanya Holiday Resort is approximately 55 km from Palakkad town. Take the Palakkad-Nemmara route and continue towards Nelliampathi. The scenic drive takes about 2 hours through winding mountain roads and offers breathtaking views.

From Coimbatore

From Coimbatore, take the route via Pollachi and Nemmara to reach Nelliampathi. The journey is approximately 90 km and takes about 3 hours. The route offers beautiful views of the Western Ghats.

Important Note

The last 15 km to Nelliampathi consists of hairpin bends and mountain roads. It's advisable to travel during daylight hours. Our staff can arrange for pickup from Nemmara if needed.

Nearest Railway Stations

The nearest major railway station is Palakkad Junction (55 km), which is well-connected to major cities across India. Alternatively, you can arrive at Thrissur Railway Station (75 km) or Coimbatore Junction (90 km).

From Railway Station

Taxis and private cabs are available from all three railway stations to Nelliampathi. The journey takes approximately 2-3 hours depending on your starting point.

Pre-arrange Transport

We recommend pre-arranging transportation from the railway station. Our resort can organize pickup services with prior notice at an additional charge.

Nearest Airports

Coimbatore International Airport (95 km) is the nearest airport, offering domestic connections to major Indian cities and limited international flights. Cochin International Airport (130 km) is an alternative with more extensive domestic and international connections.

Airport Transfers

Taxis and private cabs are available from both airports. The journey from Coimbatore takes approximately 3-3.5 hours, while from Cochin it takes about 4-4.5 hours.

Resort Pickup

For a seamless travel experience, we offer airport pickup services that can be arranged in advance. Please contact our reservations team to organize this service.
